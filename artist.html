Kristabelle Agius

Kristabelle Agius is a creative with a keen interest in art, graphic design, fashion, and photography. She enjoys expressing herself through visual media and handcrafted creations.

The Old Meets the New: Bringing 1980s Style Into Today’s Fashion Industry

This research project explores the evolving connection between 1980s fashion and contemporary style, using sewing as the primary medium and photography as a supporting tool. Women in their 60s—who were in their mid-20s during the 1980s—were interviewed for their insights into the era’s sewing techniques, fabrics, and design preferences. These insights informed a follow-up questionnaire for young women today, examining which elements of 1980s fashion they would incorporate into their personal style. The results reveal a cyclical dynamic, in which bold silhouettes, structured designs, and vibrant colours from the 1980s are selectively reimagined to align with modern aesthetics.
