NOVA is a final-year exhibition by the 2025 graduating class of the Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Arts. Bringing together the work of 30 artists, it showcases a wide range of dissertation projects; each one a reflection of our individual journeys, interests, and creative exploration.

The exhibition spans across different disciplines, from virtual reality and game design to fine art, animation, and photography. What connects us is a shared passion for pushing boundaries; whether through technology, storytelling, or reimagining the world around us.

NOVA is more than just an exhibition. It’s a moment of transition; where years of research, experimentation, and growth come together in a public space. It invites viewers into the ideas we’ve been shaping and the questions we’ve been asking. Some works are playful, others introspective or critical, but all reflect the evolving landscape of digital arts today.
